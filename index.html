About Us

Your Trusted Partner for Over 15 Years

Clockwise Risk Assessment Pty Ltd, based in Boksburg, has been a leader in industrial services and risk management for more than 15 years. Our commitment to excellence and safety has made us the preferred choice for businesses across various sectors.

We combine technical expertise with comprehensive risk assessment methodologies to deliver solutions that not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations. Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety in every project we undertake.

From refrigeration systems to environmental rehabilitation, we offer a diverse range of services tailored to your specific needs, backed by our extensive experience and industry knowledge.

Our Services

Refrigeration, Aircon & Ventilation

Professional installation, maintenance, and repair services for commercial and industrial refrigeration, air conditioning, and ventilation systems.

Domestic Repairs & Installation

Reliable home services including appliance repairs, installations, and maintenance with prompt response times and quality workmanship.

Supply of Bulk Refrigerant Gasses

Direct import and supply of high-quality refrigerant gases from China, ensuring competitive pricing and reliable delivery.

Occupational Health & Safety Management

Comprehensive Risk Assessment

Thorough risk evaluation and management solutions tailored to your business needs, identifying potential hazards and implementing effective controls.
